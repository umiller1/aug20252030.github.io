Fun Fact

– There is a slowly rising video game category called Therapeutic Games. They're made primarily to keep players engaged in healthy lifestyles and practices. Tetris is one of th earliest games that have been found to reduce intrusive memories after a traumatic event, with this research being a key leading finding for developers, healthcare, and science. Some other examples: Stardew Valley, Celeste, Unpacking, Journey, Spiritfarer, and Abzu. The genre is currently dominated by indie developers.
