Fun Fact

– The way numbers are phrased can have a huge impact on how you perceive the information. For example, if I say "only 1% of Orlando was watching your stream.", it may be seen as a very small number, but the approximate hard number of that is 3,483 people. For streamers, that still may seem small but imagine having to host that many people in a physical location. Context and relativity is important.
